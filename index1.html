Project topic: Meteaverse 101

Background

Metaverse is an augmented reality platform that enables users to build interactive experiences that blend the reality and virtual world. It embodies a virtual representation of cyberspace, providing an immersive, interactive and collaborative 3D environment. Technologies that facilitate access create decentralized and interoperable digital communities. Exploring its characteristics, the metaverse has characteristics such as boundlessness, persistence, decentralization, immersive virtual reality, virtual economy, and social interaction. The aim is to seamlessly transform real-world experiences into virtual, mixed and augmented realities, promoting a comprehensive understanding of their capabilities.It is also believed to be the next iteration of the internet.

Applications

The application of metaverse across different aspects. Elements of virtual worlds and AR/VR have been widely used in games for decades. This technology continues to evolve to create games that are more visually appealing to users and allow them to interact with their environment more naturally. Graphics and visuals continue to improve to make it seem like users are in a real-life environment. With the help of metaverse, education systems can see serveral applications in areas of study such as anatomy, biology, geography and chemistry. It also helps students learn more effectively by focusing their attention on the topic they are studying. AR and VR are already making their way into education through tools like Google Arts & Culture. Through this Google software, students can take virtual 3D tours of some of the world's most famous museums and even travel abroad without leaving the classroom.
